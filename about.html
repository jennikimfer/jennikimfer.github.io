about the coop

i've been meaning to make a personal website, but i haven't out of laziness. the catalyst for this was spite. which i guess is net good because there is less activation energy in adding to an existing site than making a new one.

this site is built with heavy help from ai. i'm not a web developer, and it can't hurt to learn a bit about web development, so here i am :)

you'll probably notice that there is a chicken theme going on. i don't have a favorite animal, and if i had to choose one, it'd be a cat. since middle school, i've just tended to follow a chicken theme. :shrug:

you'll also probably notice that my name is not jenky. i go by jennifer and jenn, but i think both those names are too common. i am extremely quirky, different, cool, awesome, epic, rad, and tubular, so i put my gamer tag instead.

also, i am a light mode lover, so this website will never get a dark mode. nobody has asked or will ask about this because nobody cares that much about this website besides me, but i just wanted a reason to say that i love light mode.
